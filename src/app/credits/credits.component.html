A few people whose work inspired elements of this page. I definitely did not just straight up copy their ideas. Totally. (Okay, maybe a little.)

Suresh Murali

Showed me how classy the contrast between pure black and white can be, if done right (cool fonts go a long way).
If it weren't for him I probably would have gone my usual emo route by slapping a dark background on with some crazy neon text and calling it a day (not that there's anything wrong with that).

Brittany Chiang

The links bar on the wide-screen version of this page is a direct rip-off of hers.
It's frickin' awesome -- all the important information (ways to view my work and to contact me) is always on screen, yet never gets in the way. So simple and effective.
